About the Preset Previewer
What is the Preset Previewer?

The Nicolesy Preset Previewer is a way for you to preview many of the LUT preset packs in the Nicolesy Store with your own photographs before you purchase them.

How do I get started?

To get started, first you will need to create a user account. Then, click the upload button at the top of the page and select a file you wish to preview. After your photo is uploaded and processed, you will be redirected to a page where you can preview the Nicolesy LUT presets with your own image.

Are my photos safe?

Yes! Any photo you upload here will only be used on this site for you to preview the preset packs. I do not and will not use your photos without your permission. Also, each time you upload a photo, the previous image is deleted and removed from the database.

How do I reset my password?

If you need to reset your password, please send me a message and I will help you out. This site is still in Beta and does not yet have the ability to allow users to change their own passwords. (Please note that your username and password on this site are different than your Nicolesy Store login.)
